SlideAccordion jQuery Plguin

Creates a content accordion that slide and scroll!

What does this plugin do?

SlideAccordion allow you to create a FAQ like chapters in your page where the content starts hidden then display when the title being clicked.
When click to another chapter a smooth scroll effect will close the opened chapter and move to the next one.

How do I use this demo?

Just start to click over chapter titles!
